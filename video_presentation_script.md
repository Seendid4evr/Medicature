# Medicature: Video Presentation Script

**Project Title:** Medicature - Digital Health & Pharmacy Platform
**Target Length:** ~8–10 Minutes
**Format:** Screen Recording with Voiceover

---

## 🎬 Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 – 0:30)

**Visual (Screen):**
Show the `login.php` page — the beautiful split-screen with the blue gradient brand panel on the left and the login card on the right.

**Voiceover:**
*"Hello everyone, and welcome to the presentation of **Medicature** — Bangladesh's most advanced digital health platform. Today, I'll walk you through every feature of this application, from the patient's experience to the powerful administrator backend. Let's get started."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 2: Patient Registration (0:30 – 1:00)

**Visual (Screen):**
Click **"Register for free"**. Fill in a name, email, and password. Point to the **password strength meter** animating in real time. Submit the form.

**Voiceover:**
*"New users can easily create a free patient account. Notice the live password strength indicator — the system enforces secure passwords right from the start. Once registered, the user is redirected back to log in."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 3: Patient Login (1:00 – 1:20)

**Visual (Screen):**
On the login page, click the **"Patient Login"** tab. Enter credentials and log in. The page redirects to the dashboard.

**Voiceover:**
*"The login page offers two modes — Patient and Admin — clearly separated by tabs. Patients enter their credentials and are taken directly to their personal health dashboard."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 4: Dashboard (1:20 – 1:50)

**Visual (Screen):**
Scroll slowly through `dashboard.php`. Point to the medication stat cards and the **"Today's Schedule"** section with upcoming doses and alarm times.

**Voiceover:**
*"The Dashboard is the heart of the patient experience. At a glance, users can see their total medicines, how many doses they've taken today, and their full schedule for the day. Browser-based alarms fire automatically at the exact time each dose is due, so patients never miss a medication."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 5: Medicine Management (1:50 – 2:30)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **💊 Medicines**. Show the list of medicines with their status badges. Click **"+ Add Medicine"**. Fill in a medicine name — the autocomplete suggests from the 21,000+ BD database. Set dosage, dates, and alarm times. Click Save.

**Voiceover:**
*"In the Medicines section, patients manage their entire prescription list. Adding a medicine is intuitive — as you type, the system autocompletes from a database of over 21,000 Bangladeshi medicines. You set the dosage, the start and end dates, and your preferred alarm times. The medicine is then tracked and scheduled automatically."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 6: Medicine Search Database (2:30 – 2:55)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **🔍 Search**. Type "Napa" in the search bar. Show the dropdown results appearing with brand name, generic name, dosage form, and manufacturer. Click a result to open its details page.

**Voiceover:**
*"The Search page taps into our offline database of 21,000-plus Bangladeshi medicines. As you type, results appear instantly — showing the brand name, the generic compound, the dosage form, and the manufacturer. Clicking any result opens a full medicine detail card."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 7: Buy Medicine (2:55 – 3:25)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **🛒 Buy Medicine**. Show the list of active prescriptions. Click the **"🛒 Buy Now"** button — it opens Arogga's website in a new tab with the medicine name pre-searched.

**Voiceover:**
*"This is one of our most exciting new features — the Buy Medicine page. The system automatically pulls all of the patient's currently active prescriptions and lists them here. With a single click on 'Buy Now,' the patient is taken directly to Arogga, our partner online pharmacy, with the medicine pre-filled in the search. Refilling a prescription has never been this fast."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 8: AI Symptom Checker (3:25 – 3:55)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **🤖 AI Triage**. Type in symptoms like *"fever and headache"*. Click Analyze. Show the AI response with suggested OTC medicines and the prominent medical disclaimer at the bottom.

**Voiceover:**
*"Our AI Triage feature is powered by a knowledge-based system. The patient describes their symptoms, and the AI suggests relevant over-the-counter medicines — for example, Paracetamol for a fever. Critically, every response includes a mandatory disclaimer encouraging the user to see a certified doctor. We prioritize safety."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 9: Health Calculators (3:55 – 4:20)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **🧮 Calculators**. Enter a sample height and weight into the BMI calculator. Click Calculate and show the result with the colored category label (e.g., "Normal weight"). Do the same for the Ideal Weight calculator.

**Voiceover:**
*"The Calculators page provides two essential health tools. The BMI calculator computes the user's Body Mass Index and color-codes the result — green for normal, yellow for underweight, red for obese. The Ideal Weight calculator uses the Devine formula to give a recommended weight range based on height and gender."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 10: Family Management (4:20 – 4:50)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **👨‍👩‍👦 Family**. Show the list of added family members/dependents. Point out that medicines can be assigned to a specific family member.

**Voiceover:**
*"The Family module allows a single account to manage the health of multiple people. A parent can add their children or elderly relatives as dependents, and then assign specific medicines and schedules to each of them. This makes Medicature ideal for family caregivers."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 11: Health Reports (4:50 – 5:20)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **📊 Reports**. Show the Current Prescriptions table and the 7-Day Adherence Summary with color-coded percentages. Point to the **"🖨️ Print Report"** button. Click it to show the browser print preview.

**Voiceover:**
*"The Reports page generates a comprehensive medication summary. It shows all current prescriptions with their generic names and schedules, and it calculates a 7-day adherence rate for each medicine — shown in green for good adherence, amber for moderate, and red for poor. The report is fully printable with a single click, designed to be brought to a doctor's appointment."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 12: Profile & Notification Settings (5:20 – 5:45)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **👤 Profile**. Show the personal information section. Then show the **Password Change form**. Finally, click **"Enable Browser Notifications"** and show the browser permission popup.

**Voiceover:**
*"In the Profile section, patients can update their name, change their password, and configure browser-based notifications. Enabling notifications ensures that even when the app is in the background, the medication alarm will still fire in the browser — keeping patients on schedule."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 13: Pharmacy Orders (5:45 – 6:10)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **🏪 Pharmacy**. Show the order history list with status badges like Pending, Delivered, etc.

**Voiceover:**
*"The Pharmacy page shows the patient's full order history. Every order placed through the platform — including the medicine name, quantity, and total cost — is recorded here with a live status badge, so patients always know where their refill order stands."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 14: Admin Login (6:10 – 6:30)

**Visual (Screen):**
Click **Logout**. On the login page, switch to the **"⚙️ Admin Login"** tab. The badge appears saying "Admin access only. Actions are logged." Enter the admin email and password and log in.

**Voiceover:**
*"Now let's see the administrator side. After logging out, I switch to the Admin Login tab. Notice the warning badge — admin access is strictly controlled. I'll now log in with the administrator credentials."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 15: Admin Dashboard (6:30 – 6:50)

**Visual (Screen):**
Show `admin/index.php` with the overview stats — total users, total active medicines, etc.

**Voiceover:**
*"The Admin Dashboard gives an instant platform-wide overview — the total number of registered patients, how many medicines are being tracked system-wide, and quick navigation to all admin tools."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 16: Patient Directory — View, Add & Delete (6:50 – 7:40)

**Visual (Screen):**
1. Navigate to **👥 Registered Patients**. Show the table of all users with their active medicines listed.
2. Click **"+ Add New Patient"** — show the form and fill it in.
3. Go back. Click **"👁️ View Details"** on a user — walk through the detail card showing: **email, raw password, family members, active medicines, and medicine history**.
4. Point to the **"🗑️ Delete"** button (but don't click it during the demo).

**Voiceover:**
*"The Patient Directory is where the admin manages all registered users. I can see at a glance what medicines each patient is currently on. I can add new patients manually using the 'Add New Patient' button. For a deep dive, I click 'View Details' on any user — and here I see their complete profile: their email, their password, their family members, and their full medicine history — both current and past."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 17: Email Notifications / Broadcast (7:40 – 8:15)

**Visual (Screen):**
Navigate to **📧 Email Broadcast**. Show the compose form. Select "All Registered Users" from the dropdown. Type a sample subject and message. Point out the target options (All Users vs. Active Users vs. a specific email).

**Voiceover:**
*"The Email Broadcast tool allows the admin to send notifications directly to patients. You can target all registered users, only users who have medicines actively tracked, or a specific patient's email. The system automatically personalizes each email with the recipient's name and wraps it in a professional Medicature-branded template."*

---

## 🎬 Scene 18: Conclusion (8:15 – 8:45)

**Visual (Screen):**
Log out of admin. Return to the beautiful split-screen login page. Let it sit on screen for 5 seconds while you wrap up.

**Voiceover:**
*"Medicature is a complete, end-to-end digital health platform. From a patient's first registration to setting smart medication alarms, running AI-powered symptom checks, buying medicines online, generating printable reports, and giving the admin total control over the platform — every feature was built with real-world healthcare needs in mind. Thank you for watching."*

---

### 📝 Recording Tips:
1. **Pre-populate your database first.** Before recording, register 2–3 dummy patients, add medicines, set alarms, and add family members so every page looks full and alive.
2. **Slow your mouse down.** Move deliberately to each feature before speaking about it.
3. **Pause on each page for 2–3 seconds** before navigating away — let the audience absorb the UI.
4. **Zoom in** on important elements like the password strength meter, the AI response, and the adherence percentages in Reports.
5. **Keep the browser in full screen** for a professional, clean recording.
